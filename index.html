Project Objective

Deliver brand-level consumer insights from Sephora skincare reviews to support portfolio decisions — covering health diagnostics, needs discovery, driver modelling, and strategic prioritisation.

Primary KPI

is_recommended (binary advocacy measure, Option A). Secondary KPI: net helpfulness score (pos_feedback - neg_feedback). All final recommendations anchored to primary KPI.

Scope

3 brands (1 focal + 2 competitors, ≥500 reviews each). Top 5 products per brand. Up to 2,000 reviews per brand, stratified by submission_time. Final dataset: ~3,000–6,000 reviews.

Submission

One ZIP per team: Report.pdf + /Code (Python scripts) + /Data (cleaned dataset). Submitted via LMO by 5:00 PM, 18 May 2026.
